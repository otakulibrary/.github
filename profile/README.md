Otaku Library is a social network for anime and manga enthusiasts, designed to bring together people with shared interests and provide a comfortable space for communication and maintaining their own 'otaku library'.

## Who we are
We are a team of passionate enthusiasts, driven by the desire to create something that truly resonates with users.

## Contributing to the project
The project is not open source, but we are always looking to grow our team. You can find our contact details in the header of our organization.
___
The organization operates on a non-profit basis.
